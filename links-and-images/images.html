Images

This page covers common image formats, but you may also be looking for links and useful extras.

JPG

JPG images are good for photos and images with lots of gradients in them

They are designed to handle large color palettes without exorbitantly increasing file size.

GIF

GIFS are good for animations

GIFS are the go-to option for simple animation but the trade off is that they're somewhat limited in terms of color palette.

PNG

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons.

PNGs are great for anything that's not a photo or animated. They deal with opacity and don't have color palette limitations but can produce larger file size for an quivalent JPG file.

SVG

SVGs are amazing. Use them wherever you can.

SVG is a vector-based graphics format, meaning it can scale up or down to any diemsnion without loss of quality.
